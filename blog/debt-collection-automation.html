How to Automate Debt Collection Without Ruining Relationships

SB

SmartBiz Strategy Team

There is a saying in Nairobi: "Kukopesha ni harusi, kulipa ni matanga." (Lending is a wedding, paying back is a funeral).

For Kenyan SMEs, cash flow is the number one killer. You deliver the goods, you do the work, and then you wait. Sending that text message asking for your money is awkward. You don't want to look desperate, but you have bills to pay.

The solution? Remove your emotions from the equation using Automation.

1. Why Humans Are Bad at Collecting Debt

When you (the business owner) call a client to ask for payment, two things happen:

  • Guilt: You feel bad asking for "your" money.
  • Negotiation: The client hears your voice and thinks, "I can explain my problems to them."

When an Automated System sends a payment reminder, the dynamic changes. People prioritize paying "systems" because systems don't have feelings. They don't listen to excuses. They just send the invoice again.

2. The "Good Cop, Bad Cop" Strategy

Effective automation uses a sequence. You don't start with threats. You start with helpfulness.

Step 1: The "Polite Nudge" (3 Days Before Due)

This message assumes they just forgot. It is friendly.

"Hi [Name], hope you're having a great week! Just a quick heads up that Invoice #102 for KES 15,000 is due on Friday. You can pay via M-Pesa Paybill 123456. Thanks!"

Step 2: The "Firm Reminder" (3 Days Overdue)

Now the tone shifts. It is strictly business.

"Hello [Name]. We haven't received payment for Invoice #102 yet. It is now 3 days overdue. Please clear this by EOD to avoid service interruption."

Step 3: The "Final Notice" (14 Days Overdue)

This is where you mention consequences.

"FINAL NOTICE: Invoice #102 is significantly overdue. If payment is not received within 48 hours, this account will be flagged for CRB listing and handed to our collections team."

3. Integrating with M-Pesa Paybill

The best time to collect money is immediately after the reminder. Your automated message should always include:

  • The exact Amount (KES).
  • The Paybill Number.
  • The Account Number.

SmartBiz tools can even generate a Payment Link. When the client clicks it, the M-Pesa menu pops up on their phone (STK Push) with the amount pre-filled. They just type their PIN.
