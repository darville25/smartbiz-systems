Inventory Management: Stop Using Notebooks & Stop 'Wizi'

SB

SmartBiz Ops Team

If you own a Hardware store in River Road, a Chemist in Estates, or a Wines & Spirits shop, you know the biggest headache isn't customers. It's Inventory Shrinkage.

In polite terms, it's called "Shrinkage." In Kenyan terms, it's called "Wizi" (Theft). Employees selling stock and pocketing the cash, or stock "mysteriously disappearing" from the shelves.

If you are still using a Counter Book to track sales and stock, you are making it easy for people to steal from you. Here is why you need to digitize in 2025.

1. The Problem with the Notebook

The "Counter Book" system relies on trust. You trust your employee to write down every sale. You trust them to do the math correctly.

Reality Check: A dishonest employee can sell an item for KES 500, write down KES 300, and keep the difference. Or they can sell it and not write it down at all.

Without a digital system, you cannot do a "Stock Reconciliation" quickly. You have to physically count everything, which takes hours.

2. How POS (Point of Sale) Systems Stop Theft

A POS system with a Barcode Scanner changes the game.

  • No Scanner, No Sale: The system forces the employee to scan the item to generate a receipt. Customers expect receipts now.
  • Real-Time Stock Levels: If the system says you have 10 Cement bags, and you count 9 physical bags, you know exactly when the theft happened.
  • User Logs: The system tracks who was logged in when the discrepancy occurred.

3. Cloud Systems vs. Offline Systems

In the past, POS systems were expensive servers that sat in the shop. Now, we use Cloud systems.

The Benefit: You can be sitting at home (or on holiday), open your phone, and see exactly what is being sold in your shop in real-time. You get an alert if a "Void" or "Refund" is processed.

4. Best Tools for Kenyan Retailers

Loyverse (Free Version Available)

Excellent for restaurants and small shops. It works on Android tablets. You can track ingredients and stock levels easily.

Odoo (For Larger Operations)

If you have a warehouse or multiple branches, Odoo is the gold standard. It connects your inventory to your accounting automatically.

Custom SmartBiz Systems

Sometimes off-the-shelf software is too complex. We build simple, custom dashboards for hardware owners that track exactly what matters to them (e.g., Cement, Paint, Mabati) without the fluff.

Conclusion

Inventory management software pays for itself in the first month just by stopping leaks. Stop running your business on paper. It's time to digitize.
